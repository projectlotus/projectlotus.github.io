Concert Choir

Concert Choir is a mixed group for all levels of experience in music. The class meets every other day for the duration of the school year. The choir performs in four concerts and will be traveling to Grace Cathedral in San Francisco during the Spring Semester.

Vocal Ensemble

Vocal Ensemble is an audition group for experienced singers. They learn advanced repertoire to perform at four concerts and on various special events. The class meets every other day for the duration of the year with a final performance at Graduation to give our Seniors a memorable farewell.

Read more

Treble Choir

Treble Choir is a beginner group consisting of altos and sopranos. Girls entering high school and choir for the first time are taught the basics of singing, sight reading, and musicality. They have the opportunity to perform in two concerts throughout the semester and a trip to the State Capitol during the holiday season.
