Frequently Asked Questions

How do I partner?

Before you contact someone, make sure you have at least 50 members. You should contact the Partnership Manager, who can be found in the Voice Moderators.

If offline, you have the option to ask in #general for a partner team member, type .partner in #bots to contact a partner team member, or contact staff through the ChillZone Bot's "DM me for help" feature.

Become a Staff Member

How do I become a Staff member?

In order to become part of ChillZone staff, you must be chosen by us.

We do not have applications for staff positions.

The entry staff position is Bot Commander, the second level is Moderator, and the third level is Administrator. All staff must meet either a 1,000 message requirement or 6h in voice channel requirement per week.
